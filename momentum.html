⚡ Momentum & Oscillators

Measure the speed and strength of price movement. Identify overbought and oversold conditions before they reverse, and spot when momentum is fading.

Momentum ★★☆ Beginner

RSI

// Relative Strength Index

Measures the speed and magnitude of recent price changes on a 0–100 scale. Above 70 = overbought. Below 30 = oversold.
